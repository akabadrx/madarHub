Important information

Important Notes

Complimentary coffee is available for selected packages during working hours and is served on request by Madar Hub staff. This benefit is intended for active members and registered users of the package.

The fixed desk monthly subscription gives the member a dedicated desk for the month. Personal items should only be left at the desk according to Madar Hub's internal rules and security policy.

Member-only events and workshops are available for active monthly members and registered private team room members.

Open/free events and workshops are available to students, day pass users, and the wider Madar Hub community, depending on event capacity and registration requirements.

Business address and mail handling services are available only for approved members or registered clients with an active subscription.

Madar Hub can receive mail and documents on behalf of approved clients. Clients will be notified when mail or documents arrive.

Madar Hub does not open, scan, forward, or act on mail unless there is clear written authorization from the client.

Clients must keep their subscription active to continue using Madar Hub's address for business purposes. If a client cancels or fails to renew, they are responsible for updating their business address with RDB, RRA, banks, partners, and any other institutions where the address is registered.

Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer coworking day passes in Kigali?

Yes, Madar Hub offers coworking day passes for 10,000 RWF per day. You get full access to the coworking space, internet, and a professional workspace for the day.

Can I rent a desk monthly at Madar Hub?

Yes, the Fixed Desk Monthly Subscription is 100,000 RWF per month and gives you your own dedicated desk, internet, coffee, and member events.

Can I use Madar Hub as a business address for RDB registration?

Yes, the Virtual Address / Business Address Support package at 100,000 RWF per month provides address authorization support, mail and document receiving, and professional address use for RDB/RRA registration. Madar Hub does not guarantee approval of any government process.

Can I rent a private room for my team?

Yes, the Private Team Room is 600,000 RWF per month and accommodates up to 6 registered members with internet, coffee, business address support, and meeting room access.

How much is the meeting room?

The meeting room is 20,000 RWF for a half-day (up to 4 hours) or 30,000 RWF for a full day (up to 8 hours). It includes internet and a Smart TV for presentations.

Can students use the space to study?

Yes, the Student Meeting Room Day Pass is 3,000 RWF per day and provides an affordable, quiet study space with internet access.

Can I book through WhatsApp?

Yes, you can book any package through WhatsApp at 0783 662 543. Just send a message specifying which service you are interested in.
