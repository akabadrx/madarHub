Frequently asked questions

Meeting Room Rental FAQ

How much is the meeting room at Madar Hub?

The meeting room at Madar Hub costs 20,000 RWF for a half-day (up to 4 hours) and 30,000 RWF for a full-day (up to 8 hours). Both options include internet access, Smart TV, and a professional environment.

Can I book the meeting room for a half-day?

Yes, you can book the meeting room for a half-day at 20,000 RWF, which gives you up to 4 hours of private meeting room access. This is ideal for short meetings, interviews, or focused work sessions.

Is there a TV for presentations in the meeting room?

Yes, the meeting room at Madar Hub has a Smart TV available for presentations and projection. You can connect your device to display slides, reports, or any visual content during your meeting.

Is internet available in the meeting room?

Yes, reliable internet access is included with every meeting room booking. You can use the internet for online calls, video conferences, research, presentations, or any other work that requires a stable connection.

Can I book the meeting room for interviews?

Yes, the meeting room is well-suited for interviews. It provides a private, professional, and quiet environment for job interviews, client meetings, and one-on-one discussions.

How do I book the meeting room?

You can book the meeting room by messaging Madar Hub on WhatsApp at 0783 662 543. Just send a message with your preferred date and time, and we will confirm your booking and answer any questions you have.
