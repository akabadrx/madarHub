Frequently asked questions

Coworking Day Pass FAQ

Do you offer coworking day passes in Kigali?

Yes, Madar Hub offers a coworking day pass for 10,000 RWF per day. The day pass gives you access to our shared workspace in Kimironko, Kigali with reliable internet, a professional environment, a comfortable workstation, and coffee on request.

Can I use the coworking space for just one day?

Yes, the coworking day pass is designed for flexible one-day use. There is no monthly commitment required. You can book a day pass whenever you need a productive workspace, whether it is for a single day or scattered days throughout the month.

Is internet available at Madar Hub?

Yes, reliable internet access is included with every coworking day pass. You can use the internet for remote work, online meetings, research, studying, or any other work that requires a stable connection.

Is coffee available at the coworking space?

Yes, coffee is served on request at Madar Hub, subject to availability. Simply ask our staff and they will prepare it for you during working hours.

Where is Madar Hub located?

Madar Hub is located on KG 42 Street in Kimironko, Gasabo, Kigali, Rwanda. From the western or back entrance of Kimironko Four Square Church, head south along the north-south road for a few blocks until you see the Madar Hub sign.

Can I book a coworking day pass through WhatsApp?

Yes, you can book a coworking day pass by messaging Madar Hub on WhatsApp at 0783 662 543. Just send a message and we will confirm your booking and answer any questions you have.
