Yes, Madar Hub offers a business address support service that allows you to use our physical address in Kimironko, Kigali for business registration and official correspondence. The service includes address authorization support and costs 100,000 RWF per month.

Yes, the virtual address service provides physical address support for RDB and RRA registration. Madar Hub provides address authorization support and the use of our address for registration purposes, but does not guarantee approval of any registration, amendment, tax, bank, or legal process.

Yes, Madar Hub receives mail and documents on behalf of approved clients with an active subscription. You will be notified when mail or documents arrive. Madar Hub does not open, scan, forward, or act on mail unless there is clear written authorization from the client.
