Frequently asked questions

Private Team Room FAQ

Can I rent a private team room at Madar Hub?

Yes, Madar Hub offers a Private Team Room for 600,000 RWF per month. It is a dedicated private room that can accommodate up to 6 registered members, with internet access, business address support, meeting room access, coffee, and member events.

How many members can use the Private Team Room?

The Private Team Room can accommodate up to 6 registered members. All team members are registered with Madar Hub and receive full access to the included benefits such as internet, coffee, member events, and workshops.

What is included in the Private Team Room package?

The Private Team Room package includes a dedicated private room, capacity for up to 6 registered members, internet access, coffee served on request for registered members subject to availability, use of Madar Hub's physical address for business registration support, physical address support for RDB/RRA registration and official business correspondence, business registration address support for the team or company, mail and document receiving, notification when mail or documents arrive, access to the meeting room for team meetings subject to availability and fair-use scheduling, and access to member-only and open events and workshops.

Is there meeting room access with the Private Team Room?

Yes, Private Team Room members have access to the meeting room for team meetings, subject to availability and fair-use scheduling. You can book the meeting room when you need a separate space for team discussions, client meetings, or presentations.

Is a business address included with the Private Team Room?

Yes, the Private Team Room package includes business address support. You can use Madar Hub's physical address in Kimironko, Kigali for RDB and RRA registration, official business correspondence, mail and document receiving, and address authorization support for your team or company.

How do I book the Private Team Room at Madar Hub?

You can book the Private Team Room by messaging Madar Hub on WhatsApp at 0783 662 543. Send a message expressing your interest and we will confirm availability, schedule a visit, and help you get started with your private team room subscription.
