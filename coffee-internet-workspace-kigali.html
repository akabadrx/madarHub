Yes, coffee is served on request at Madar Hub, subject to availability. Simply ask our staff and they will prepare it for you during working hours. This is included with the Coworking Day Pass and the Fixed Desk Monthly Subscription.

How is the internet at Madar Hub?

Madar Hub provides reliable internet access designed for remote work, online meetings, research, studying, and other online tasks. Our connection is stable and suited for professional use, so you can work without interruptions.

Madar Hub is a professional workspace, not a cafe. Unlike cafes, Madar Hub offers a quiet, focused environment without loud music, crowds, or distractions. You get reliable internet, proper workstations, and coffee on request without the noise and interruptions typical of cafes.

Yes, Madar Hub is designed as a quiet, professional workspace. Unlike cafes and other public spaces, our environment is set up to minimize distractions so you can focus on your work, study, or online meetings.
