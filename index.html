Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Madar Hub offer?

Madar Hub offers coworking day passes, fixed monthly desks, virtual business address support for RDB/RRA registration, private team rooms, meeting room rental, training room rental, and an affordable student study space in Kimironko, Kigali.

Where is Madar Hub located?

Madar Hub is located on KG 42 Street, Kimironko, Gasabo, Kigali, Rwanda, near Kimironko Sector Office and Four Square Church.

Can I book through WhatsApp?

Yes, you can book any service through WhatsApp at 0783 662 543. Just send a message and we will assist you.

Is coffee available at Madar Hub?

Yes, coffee is available on request for coworking, fixed desk, and private team room members, subject to availability. Meeting room and training room coffee may have additional fees.

Can I use Madar Hub as a business address for RDB registration?

Yes, Madar Hub provides business address support for RDB and RRA registration, including address authorization, mail receiving, and document handling. Please note that Madar Hub does not guarantee approval of any government or institutional process.

Can I visit before subscribing?

Absolutely. You are welcome to visit Madar Hub before committing. Contact us on WhatsApp at 0783 662 543 to schedule a visit.
