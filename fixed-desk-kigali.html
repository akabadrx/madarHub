Frequently asked questions

Fixed Desk Monthly Subscription FAQ

Can I rent a desk monthly at Madar Hub?

Yes, Madar Hub offers a fixed desk monthly subscription for 100,000 RWF per month. You get your own dedicated desk in our Kimironko, Kigali workspace with internet access, a professional environment, coffee on request, and access to member-only events and workshops.

What is included in the fixed desk monthly subscription?

The fixed desk subscription includes your own dedicated desk for the month, monthly coworking access, reliable internet, a quiet professional workspace, coffee served on request subject to availability, and access to member-only events and workshops at Madar Hub.

Can I leave my personal items at my fixed desk?

The fixed desk monthly subscription gives you a dedicated desk for the month. Personal items should only be left at the desk according to Madar Hub's internal rules and security policy. Please contact us for details about what can be stored at your desk.

Is coffee available with the fixed desk subscription?

Yes, coffee is served on request at Madar Hub, subject to availability. Simply ask our staff and they will prepare it for you during working hours.

Where is Madar Hub located?

Madar Hub is located on KG 42 Street in Kimironko, Gasabo, Kigali, Rwanda. From the western or back entrance of Kimironko Four Square Church, head south along the north-south road for a few blocks until you see the Madar Hub sign.

Can I book a fixed desk through WhatsApp?

Yes, you can subscribe to a fixed desk by messaging Madar Hub on WhatsApp at 0783 662 543. Just send a message and we will confirm your subscription and answer any questions you have.
