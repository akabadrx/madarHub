How much is the training room at Madar Hub?

The training room at Madar Hub costs 40,000 RWF per day. This includes room access for the day, internet access, whiteboard, Smart TV for presentations and projection, normal chairs, a professional training environment, and 10 cups of coffee.

What equipment is available in the training room?

The training room at Madar Hub comes equipped with a whiteboard for writing and teaching, a Smart TV for presentations and projection, reliable internet access, and normal chairs. It is set up to support workshops, training programs, small classes, presentations, and seminars.

Yes, Madar Hub's training room is specifically designed for workshops, training programs, small classes, presentations, and seminars. You can book the room for a day and run your workshop with whiteboard, Smart TV, internet, and coffee included.
